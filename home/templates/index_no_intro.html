Print Technologies

  • Photo Print

    Colour papers require specific chemical processing in proprietary chemicals. Today's processes are called RA-4, which is for printing colour negatives, and Ilfochrome, for colour transparencies. We are using the latest available technologies to guarantee the highest possible quality. UltraHD on 300 gsm glossy paper for you lasting wallart

  • Canvas Prints

    Reproductions of original artwork have been printed on canvas for many decades using offset printing. Since the 1990s, canvas print has been also been associated with either dye sublimation or inkjet print processes. Our canvas print material is cotton based poly canvas.

    printers capable of producing canvas prints range from small consumer printers up to large format printers capable of printing onto canvas rolls measuring 1.5 metres (59 in) or more, therefore we are able to print wide variety of sizes.

  • Metal Print

    Full-color dye sublimated metal print for the most vivd experience. The 'metallic canvas' in your lounge gives the ultimate modern art feeling to your home.

  • Acrylic Print

    Your photo will be printed on high grade photo paper and reverse mounted to acrylic. 

  • Framed Print

    Your selected image will be printed with our state of the art photo printer and placed on passepartout. Classic art on your wall.
